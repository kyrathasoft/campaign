Unconverted:: Between the two of them, the lizardman — who attacked Aury and Mattie in the bog on the 22 Oct. 204 PR — have thirteen chroma, no royals, and a dozen red cuprous copper pieces. The thirteen chroma, when spent appropriately, will convert to (1.3)(0.000001) = 0.0000013 experience. They'll probably not be in a city when they get around to converting the red cuprous, so we're therefore talking about 65 of these coins converting to a single royal. The red cuprous (converted to 0.1846 gold) can be spent for (0.1846)(0.000001) = 0.00000001846 experience. That'll need to be split two-ways: 0.00000000923 experience.


 
 
 
 

Unconverted: Electrum pieces carried by individual froglings that attacked Matthias and Aury in the Bog on 22 Oct. 204 PR: 7, 5, 3, 8, 9, 2, 2, 1, 7, 7. This totals 51 EP — electrum is rare, a currency of fallen kingdoms.

Electrum is an alloy of silver and gold, and prized by magic-users as a material from which to construct wands and rings. Our heroes may be able to get more than the going market-rate conversion for these coins of fallen kingdoms, should they seek out various magic-users.

These 51 electrum pieces are 25.5 gp-worth of treasure, which will translate — when appropriately spent — to (25.5)(0.000001) equals 0.0000255 (i.e., two-hundred fifty-five millionths) experience — though see the previous paragraph. Of course, Matthias and Aury will each get only half of that amount, 0.00001275 (one-thousand two-hundred and seventy-five millionths) when the treasure is appropriately spent.
