Scarlet Horizons

Terminology

 

Auriochos d'Rosenberger — A cleric of Helix and a member of The Seekers, a brotherhood that works to reclaim the lost knowledge of Kyrathaba's previous ages, even back in the dimmest past when it was called Earth.

 

Chudwick — Cimmer the Glassblower's boy in the village of Helix, region of Stigrix. An eight-year old human with multiple vitamin deficiencies who is weak and poorly and has garnered Matthias' compassion.

 

Cimmer Castion — Chudwick's father and master glassblower of Helix.

 

Fornost d'Voivodja — The abbot who founded the Seekers in Pre-Ravaging 102, in Helix.

 

gnosis — any truth or accepted fact within the Scarlet Horizons game setting that is intuitively apprehended by certain characters and monsters. To the meta-thinking players, game gnosis may be flavorful and/or tongue-in-cheek, but it is reality itself to those in-game personae who perceive it.

 

Hurg Scarface — chief of the Scarface goblin tribe that resides south of Helix at the edges of the bog.

metacept — any concept within the Scarlet Horizons game that is relevant only to player-knowledge and is non-existent as far as the players' characters are concerned.
