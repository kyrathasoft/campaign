Shrine of St. Ygg

Shrine of St. Ygg The Shrine of St. Ygg is a small church in the center of the village. Most villagers in Helix pray to both the ancient gods (Anganach) and the new (Futurus). The Shrine of St.Ygg, in particular, has been successful in establishing a small religious following. Brother Othar (Level 5 Cleric), and his under-clerics Cella (Level 2 Cleric) and Gamdar (Level 2 Half-Orc Cleric), oversee the shrine and tend to the needs of the villagers regardless of their faith.
