Lanky Campaign

Party Magic Items

+1 Boot Knife (carried by Celaena)

This boot knife confers a +1 bonus to both to-hit rolls and damage rolls when being wielded. Additionally, on the base 1d4 damage die roll, the die explodes. This means that if the die comes up '4', it's rolled again, with the next result being added to the original '4'. If the second roll also comes up a '4', you now have a running result of '8' and will roll a third time. As long as the result comes up '4', you keep rolling.
