Scarlet Horizons

Orc

• No. Enc: 2d4 (3d10)
 • Alignment: Chaotic
 • Movement: 30'
 • Armor Class: 7
 • Hit Dice: 3+3 (typically 14-17 HP)
 • BAB +4
 • Attacks: by weapon
 • Damage: By single weapon attack, +2 to damage
 • Save: F3
 • Morale: 10
 • Hoard: 2d6 chroma, 1d4-1 royals, 1d4+1 copper (25% it's Red Cuprous)

 Experience Yielded: 85 per orc defeated

Anytime ten or more orcs are found together, one of them will be a 4+3 HD specimen with 31 HP with 1d4+3 royals, 2d4+4 chroma, 1d4+4 copper (15% it's Red Cuprous). This leader will have the same Morale and saving throws as its fellows. A defeated leader orc is worth 105 xp.

Orcs are bullies in groups, much less confident on their own. They are quite cruel.
