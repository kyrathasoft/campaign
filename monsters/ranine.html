Ranine

• No. Enc: 2d4 (3d10)
 • Alignment: Chaotic
 • Movement: 30'
 • Armor Class: 7
 • Hit Dice: 2+1 (typically 10-13 HP)
 • BAB +3
 • Attacks: by weapon
 • Damage: By single weapon attack, +1 to damage
 • Save: F3
 • Morale: 10
 • Hoard: 2d6 chroma, 1d4-1 royals, 1d4+1 copper (25% it's Red Cuprous)

 Experience Yielded: ?? per ranine defeated

Anytime ten or more ranine are found together, one of them will be a 3+3 HD specimen with 21 HP with 1d4-1 royals, 2d4+4 chroma, 1d4+6 copper (25% it's Red Cuprous). This leader will have the same Morale and saving throws as its fellows. A defeated leader ranine is worth 125 xp.

The frog-like ranine serve the demon lord Tsath-Dagon.
