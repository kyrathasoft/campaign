Scarlet Horizons

Ranine


• No. Enc: 2d4 (3d10)
 • Alignment: Chaotic
 • Movement: 30'
 • Armor Class: 7
 • Hit Dice: 4 (typically 20-23 HP)
 • BAB +4
 • Attacks: 1d6/1d6/1d4 + paralysis, or by weapon + 1
 • SA: paralysis with successful bite unless save vs DC 11 • Save: F4
 • Morale: 10
 • Hoard: 3d6 chroma, 1d4+5 royals, 2d4+2 copper (45% it's Red Cuprous; otherwise, 25% it's Dark Cuprous)

 Experience Yielded: 300 per ranine defeated

Anytime ten or more ranine are found together, one of them will be a 4+3 HD specimen with 27 HP with the same treasure as other ranine. A defeated leader ranine is worth 325 xp.

The frog-like ranine serve the demon lord Tsath-Dagon. Ranine don't look very formidable, but they have three attacks per round, one of which has the possibility of paralyzing the victim. A paralyzed victim make re-try a saving throw in subsequent rounds to throw off the effect, with a cumulative +1 bonus.
